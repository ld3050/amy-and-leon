Getting there:

From Brisbane
Yandina Station is a 1.5 hour drive from Brisbane via the Bruce Highway (M1). Take the exit towards Route 11/Yandina-Coolum Road/Yandina/Coolum Beach. Turn left onto Valdora Road, then right on to North Arm-Yandina Creek Road.

Accomodation:

We are recommending that friends and family find accomodation in Coolum if you want to stay close to the action.

If there is enough interest we will arrange for a shuttle to take you to and from the venue. We'll let you know closer to the date.
